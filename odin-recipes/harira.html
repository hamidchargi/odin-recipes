Ma9adir Hrira

Base

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 200g beef or lamb cut tiny (or 1 chicken thigh) — optional, you can skip for veggie
  • 1 large onion, grated or very finely chopped
  • 3 large ripe tomatoes, blended (or 400g canned crushed tomatoes)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste mixed with 1 cup water

    • Legumes & herbs

  • 1 handful dry lentils (about ¾ cup) — green or brown, rinsed
  • 1 can chickpeas (240g drained) — or 1 cup pre-cooked
  • 1 small bunch parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 small bunch cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk with leaves, finely chopped (or a handful of Chinese celery)

    • Spices

  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 1½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ¼ tsp cinnamon (optional but traditional — just a pinch)

    • To finsh

    3 tbsp broken vermicelli or 2 tbsp raw rice 3 tbsp flour mixed with 1 cup cold water (tedouira) lemon wedges, dates on the side
